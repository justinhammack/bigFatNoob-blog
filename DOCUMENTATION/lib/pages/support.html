Support

Technical support

We provide technical support for this theme through Themeforest. Simply sign into your account at Themeforest and send us an email using the contact form in the sidebar of our Themeforest profile page: http://themeforest.net/user/rascals. Either way, our developers will be happy to help you.

Support rules

Support requests are handled through email only.

Support requests are handled through email only, which should be sent from ThemeForest profile page (bottom of the right column). Please include the theme name, theme version and website installation URL . We usually respond to support requests within 48 hours on weekdays, depending on the number of requests in queue. Support request sent during weekends or our holidays will be processed on Monday or the next business day.

Please also check your email spam box, sometimes our reply is in the spam box.

Support will be provided when:

  • The Issue/Question is about theme usage and configuration
  • You find a bug in the theme
  • The theme is not working on your server
  • It is a regular WordPress Question

Support will NOT be provided when:

  • You want Feature X to be integrated into the theme
  • You want to modify the theme to suit your specific needs
